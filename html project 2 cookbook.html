ğŸ¥£ Idli

Prep Time: 8 hours
Cook Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup urad dal
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak rice and dal separately for 4 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter, mix, and ferment overnight.
  3. Pour into idli molds and steam for 10 minutes.

ğŸ› Sambar

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato
  • Mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp sambar powder
  • Tamarind extract
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves, hing

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook dal until soft.
  2. Boil vegetables with tamarind and sambar powder.
  3. Add dal and temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.

ğŸµ Rasam

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 tomato
  • 1 tsp rasam powder
  • Tamarind extract
  • Mustard seeds, garlic, curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions:

  1. Boil tamarind water with tomato and rasam powder.
  2. Temper with mustard seeds, garlic, and curry leaves.
  3. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

ğŸš Ven Pongal

Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup raw rice
  • 1/4 cup moong dal
  • Ghee, pepper, cumin, ginger
  • Curry leaves, cashews

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice and dal together until mushy.
  2. Temper spices in ghee and mix into pongal.
  3. Serve hot with chutney or sambar.
